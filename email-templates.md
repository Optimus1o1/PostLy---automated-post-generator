## Using These Templates in Make.com

In your Gmail module, paste the template in the Body field. Make.com will auto-replace:
- `{{2.ClientEmail}}` → Client's actual email
- `{{2.ClientName}}` → Client's name from form
- `{{2.BusinessName}}` → Their business name
- `{{6.webViewLink}}` → Google Doc URL
- `{{2.Platform}}` → Their platform choice

Test with dummy data first before going live!

## Email Best Practices

✅ DO:
- Personalize with client names
- Include clear CTAs (call-to-action)
- Keep tone friendly and professional
- Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
- Follow up after 7 days if no response
- Include P.S. for bonus engagement

❌ DON'T:
- Use ALL CAPS (looks like shouting)
- Send too many emails (max 1 per week)
- Be pushy about renewals
- Forget to proofread
- Use generic templates
- Ignore customer feedback
