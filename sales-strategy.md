# Sales & Acquisition Strategy

Your 5-day plan to get your first paying client.

---

## Day 1: In-Person Free Demos

**Goal:** Showcase the product live, generate word-of-mouth

**Action Items:**
- [ ] Identify 3-5 local businesses near you (cafes, salons, coaching centers, consultants)
- [ ] Walk in or call with this pitch:

> "Hey! I built an AI tool that creates a full month of Instagram/social posts in minutes. 
> No charge - I just want to show you what it can do. Takes 5 minutes."

**During Demo:**
1. Ask: Business name, niche, target audience, 5 topics, tone preference
2. Fill in the Google Form (or Sheet) right there
3. Wait for Make.com to process (~2 minutes)
4. Show them the Google Doc with 30 posts
5. Their reaction = Your sales pitch

**After Demo:**
- [ ] Leave them your landing page link
- [ ] Ask: "If this cost ₹999/month, would you use it?"
- [ ] Get their number for follow-up
- [ ] Take a photo of the Google Doc for portfolio

**Success Metric:** At least 1 says "Yes, I'll pay for it"

## Day 3: Instagram DM Blitz

**Goal:** Direct outreach to business owners

**Action Items:**

### Find Target Businesses:
1. Search Instagram for local businesses in your city
2. Filter: Small to medium businesses (100-5,000 followers)
3. Industries: Cafes, gyms, consultants, e-commerce, coaching

### DM Template:
```
Hey [BusinessName]! 👋

I noticed your content is a bit inconsistent on Instagram 
(totally normal - content creation takes FOREVER).

I've been working on something that might help... I built an AI 
tool that generates an entire month of posts automatically.

Thinking of soft-launching with 3 early adopters who want 
professional content at ₹999/month.

Want a completely free sample? No obligation, just takes 2 minutes 
and you'll see if it works for you.

Let me know! 🚀
```

**Send to:** 20 local businesses

**Response Handling:**
- [ ] If "Yes" → Send Google Form link → Process immediately → Get payment
- [ ] If "Maybe" → Follow up in 2 days → Share a sample doc
- [ ] If "No thanks" → Respond politely, leave them your link

**Success Metric:** 10%+ response rate = 2-3 demo requests

## Day 5: Follow-Up & Closing

**Goal:** Convert interested prospects into paying customers

**Action Items:**

### Organize Your Prospects:
```
Status Column:
- Demo Watched (follow up TODAY)
- Interested (follow up with sample)
- Not Responding (try 1 more time)
- Ready to Buy (send payment link)
```

### Follow-Up Sequences:

**For "Demo Watched" (within 24 hours):**
```
Hey [Name]! 👋

Just checking in - what did you think of the sample content we 
generated? 

Any questions before you get started?

Here's where to sign up: [Payment Link]

Look forward to working with you! 🚀
```

**For "Interested" (with sample doc):**
```
Hi [Name],

Following up on our conversation. Here's a sample I generated 
for a [similar business] in [their niche].

Ready to see something customized for your business? 
Just fill this form and I'll generate yours: [Form Link]

Let me know if you have any questions!
```

**For "Not Responding" (1 follow-up):**
```
Hey [Name],

Haven't heard back from you! Just wanted to make sure you got my 
message about the social media content tool.

No pressure - but if you're curious, here's more info: [Link]

Hope to chat soon!
```

### Close the Sale:
- [ ] When someone says "YES":
  1. Send Razorpay payment link: [Your Link]
  2. Once payment confirmed, send Google Form link
  3. Process their request that same day
  4. Send Google Doc within 2 hours
  5. Get testimonial

**Success Metric:** ₹999 received = 1 paying customer! 🎉

### Day after First Customer:
- [ ] Process their content request
- [ ] Send polished Google Doc
- [ ] Follow up: "How does it look?"
- [ ] Get their feedback
- [ ] Ask for referral: "Know anyone else who might benefit?"

### Week 2:
- [ ] Target: 2-3 total customers
- [ ] Ask first customer for testimonial
- [ ] Take screenshot of success
- [ ] Refine your pitch based on feedback

### Week 3:
- [ ] Post success story on LinkedIn
- [ ] Update landing page with testimonials
- [ ] Increase outreach (DM 20 more businesses)
- [ ] Ask: "Can you introduce me to someone?"

### Week 4:
- [ ] Goal: 5 customers or confirmed payment from 5
- [ ] Celebrate! 🎉
- [ ] Plan hiring (sales person on commission)

Once you have 1 customer, incentivize referrals:

### Referral Offer:
- Customer refers business X
- Business X signs up: You give referrer ₹200 credit
- Cap: 10 referrals per customer per month

## When You Hit Pause

If you're not getting traction after Day 5:
1. Adjust your pitch (not the price)
2. Find a different target audience
3. Improve the demo/sample
4. Ask existing prospects for feedback

Remember: First customer is hardest. After that, referrals take over.
